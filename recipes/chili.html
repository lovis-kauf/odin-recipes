Lovis's favorite Chili con Carne (w Beans)!

Description

This is Lovis Chili con carne (and beans). It's a quick meal to feed a lot of hungry people.

Ingredients

  • 500g Ground Beef
  • 2 Onion - chopped
  • 1 Can of chopped tomatoes
  • 2 cans of red beans
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 3 oz of chili spice mix

Steps

  1. In a large pan warm a trickle of olive oil. Add chopped onions and glaze for 3 minutes.
  2. Add ground beef, add salt and chili spice mix to the meat. Brown until the meat is cooked all the way.
  3. Add one tomato can to the meat. Let simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add more spices, salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Open bean cans. Pour beans into a strainer and rinse with cold water.
  6. 20 minutes before serving add the red beans and stir them in gently.
  7. The beans shouldn't cook, they should just warm up in the chili.
  8. Serve in bowls with sour cream and cheese.
