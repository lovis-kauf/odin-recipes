Rocky's favorite Tea!

Description

Rocky prefers to drink water. But when he has a cough we make him this tea and it always helps.

Ingredients

  • Sidroga Hustentee for Kids
  • 1 cup of boiling water
  • Honey to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water in kettle to 100C.
  2. Pour water into ceramic cup. Add the tea bag and steep for 15 minutes.
  3. Add honey to taste.
  4. Make sure the tea is cooled down enough and serve to child.
